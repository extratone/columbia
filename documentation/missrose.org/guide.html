Guide

Welcome to the guide for using Miss Rose! The menu on the right lists all the sections available to you. If you're just looking to refresh your memory, then click the section related to what you're looking for. However, we do recommend that users new to Rose start from the begining and work through the guide. It's been written specifically to guide you through learning Rose's features properly and help you learn things in order, as certain commands require knowledge of other commands first.

If you find any mistakes in this guide, or feel we've missed something, please let us know by visiting @RoseSupport!

Cheers, and good luck!
