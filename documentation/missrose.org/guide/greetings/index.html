Greetings

Rose has a fully customizable module for greetings! You can have Rose greet users with a warm welcome, or bid them adieu with a fond farewell, or even both! Or neither. The choice is up to you. Your users will probably appreciate a warm welcome to your channel though. :)

Welcomes

This is where you can configure how Rose will greet new users to your group. A friendly hello, or a sinister warning to all those who enter? You decide!

Goodbyes

Just like welcomes, you can also configure how Rose will say goodbye to users who leave of their own accord. They won't be able to see what Rose says, so maybe she'll be a little cheeky ;)

Advanced Greeting Settings

In addition to the basic goodbye and welcome settings, there are a few more advanced settings that Rose has available, which can help you control spam and help keep your group chats neat and clean.
