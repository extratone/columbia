Adding Rose

Adding Rose to your Telegram group is incredibly easy!

Instructions:

  1. Open up your Telegram client of choice
  2. Do a user search for user @MissRose_bot (see note below)
  3. View Rose's profile, and click the 3-button menu in the top right corner and select the “Add to group” option, then choose your group.
  4. Congratulations! You've added Miss Rose to your group
NoteDuring Step #2, you may see other bots or users with similar names or usernames. Make sure you only add the bot with the username @MissRose_bot, others are imposters!

Alternative Instructions:

  1. Click the following link: http://telegram.me/MissRose_bot?startgroup=botstart
  2. If you're on your mobile device, the link should open your Telegram client to select the chat you wish to add Rose to.
  3. If you're on a PC, you need to have Telegram Desktop installed. When the link opens in your browser, Telegram will ask you to allow it to open the link. Allow it to open and you will get a prompt to choose which group you'd like to add Rose to.
